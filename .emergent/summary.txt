- **Initial Request:** Build a simple CRM for doctors. Features include: no login for end-users (only for admin/staff), Doctor management (Name, Reg.no, Address, Mail id, Phone Number, lead status), individual customer codes, and SMTP mail functionality.

- **Iteration 8:** Customize the WhatsApp order confirmation message to be personalized with the doctor's name, company details, and the submitter's IP/location.
- **Iteration 9:** Add transport management and order tracking to the admin panel. This includes managing a list of transport providers, updating order status (e.g., 'To Pay'/'Paid'), and associating a transporter and tracking number with an order.
