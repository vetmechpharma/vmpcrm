The user is building a comprehensive business management CRM for pharmaceutical distributors. **Recent User Request:**
1.  **Role-Based Registration:** During registration, after OTP verification, the user should be prompted to select their role (Doctor, Medical, or Agency). The registration form should then dynamically display fields relevant to that role, matching the fields available in the admin panel.

4.  **Mobile-First Design:** The customer portal should have a mobile-responsive, app-like feel, as users will primarily access it on their phones.
